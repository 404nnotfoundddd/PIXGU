## Welcome to Pixgu, where creativity meets fun!

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share your personal information through our services. It’s important that you read this entire policy, but here’s a summary to get you started:

- We care a lot about privacy. We're committed to creating a space where people can draw, guess, and have fun with friends online. Respecting user privacy is key to our mission.

- Due to the peer-to-peer technology in our service, no one, including Pixgu, can collect and process any information you make in the game room hosted by users.

- We limit required information. We only ask for what's necessary to create your account, maintain our services, and meet legal requirements.

- We don't sell your personal information. Our business is based on purchases and donates, not selling your data to third parties.

## The information we collect

We collect certain information when you use Pixgu. This includes information you provide to us, information we collect automatically, and information we receive from other sources.

- ##### Information you provide to us

  - **Account information:** When you create a Pixgu account, you only need to choose a username. If you're creating an account via an OAuth provider, depending on the provider, we may receive additional information such as your email address and/or phone number, but this is not required for account creation on our website.

  - **Content you create:** This includes any content that you upload to the service, except for content in game rooms hosted by users due to the peer-to-peer technology. For example, you may changed your profile photo. You may create game rooms, which contain options you choose. You may also upload and share files through the services.

  - **Payment information.** If you buy any paid services through Pixgu, you may need to submit a valid payment method and associated billing information, including your full name and billing address. Our payment processors, like Stripe, receive and process your payment information. Depending on the processor, we may also receive and store certain billing information, including the last four digits of the credit card number associated with the transaction. If you sell something through Pixgu, you may be required to provide additional information to Pixgu or to our payment processors, such as bank account information to facilitate payments, tax identification information, and copies of government issued identification required to identify users in accordance with regulatory obligations. If we decide to process our own payments in the future, we would receive and process this information ourselves.

  - **Information from actions you take:** We collect information about your use of Pixgu, except for content in game rooms hosted by users due to the peer-to-peer technology. This includes information such as the friends you add, the users the rooms you are in, information about purchases or sales you make on or through Pixgu including what you purchased or sold, and other related actions.

  - **Information from optional features:** Certain features, like language selection, are optional. This also includes third-party integrations you choose to enable and the data you authorize those third-party services to share with us. For example, when you link a music or video streaming account, we may collect information about that account such as the song or video you are listening to or watching in order to display that information on your profile or as your status (if you have chosen to do so).

  - **Other information you provide directly to us:** You may have the option to submit additional information as you use Pixgu. For example, you may participate in surveys where you can provide feedback on the services.

  We may receive information about you from other sources, including from other users and third parties, and combine that information with the other information we have about you. For example, if you interact with our social media account on another platform, we may receive certain information about you like your username on that platform.

## How we use your information

- **To provide you with the services:** We use your information to provide you with the Pixgu services. For example, We use your account information to connect you to game rooms. When you enable optional features (like connecting your Pixgu account to other platforms), we use information from those services to power the feature (like displaying what song you are listening to on another service).

- **To maintain a positive community:** We are committed to making Pixgu a fun, safe, and inclusive environment for all players. To achieve this, we use your information to monitor and take action against users and content that violate our Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, and other policies. Such as responding to player reports, detecting fraud and cheating attempts and proactively scanning user-generated content for inappropriate or harmful material

- **To contact you:** We use your information to contact you in connection with your account, such as contact you about important product or policy changes and to send you information about products you have purchased.

- **To provide technical support:** We use your information to respond to your issues and provide assistance. This helps us assist you with various aspects of Pixgu, such as: Addressing issues with balances, investigating bugs or glitches in game rooms, clarifying rules or gameplay features and resolving account-related concerns

- **To report on our business performance:** We use your information to track the fundamental metrics of our business, to perform financial reporting, to respond to regulatory obligations, and to debug billing issues.

- **To protect our services:** We use your information to keep our services secure, to prevent misuse, and to enforce our Terms of Service and other policies against users who violate them.

- **To protect someone’s vital interests**

We may collect or share personal data if we think someone’s life is in danger—for example, to help resolve an urgent medical situation.

## Who can see your data?

- #### With our vendors.

  We may provide information to vendors we hire to carry out specific work for us. This includes our payment processors like Stripe, which handles transactions when you make in-game purchases. We also work with Xata, our database provider, to store account information.

- #### When you tell us to:

  When you add your content to the services, you are telling us to make that content available to certain people or communities. Who can access that information is determined by who can access a particular space. For game rooms, your username and profile photo are visible to everyone in the room, including the host.

## Deleting account

You can delete your account via the Account Settings menu. Deleting your account permanently deletes identifying information and anonymizes other data as described in our data retention policy.

## Data retention

Pixgu only deletes the account and information of users who register as guests after 2 weeks of inactivity.

## Childrens

Pixgu is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not attempt to login for Pixgu or send any personal information about yourself to us. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that a child under 13 may have provided us personal information, please contact us at pixguu@gmail.com. We encourage parents and legal guardians to monitor their children's Internet usage and to help enforce our age restriction by instructing their children never to provide personal information through Pixgu. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child under 13 has obtained an account on Pixgu, please alert us at pixguu@gmail.com and request that we delete that child's personal information from our systems.
