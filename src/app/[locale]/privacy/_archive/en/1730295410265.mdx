## Welcome to Pixgu, where creativity meets fun!

In this Privacy Policy, "Service,", "Services,", "Pixgu", "PIXGU", "we,", "us," and "our" refer to the Pixgu drawing and guessing game platform and its related services provided at pixgu.com and related subdomains. "You" and "your" refer to the person using our Services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share your personal information through our services. It’s important that you read this entire policy, but here’s a summary to get you started:

- We care a lot about privacy. We're committed to creating a space where people can draw, guess, and have fun with friends online. Respecting user privacy is key to our mission.

- Due to our implementation of peer-to-peer technology, all data transfers between participants are direct and encrypted using AES encryption and cuid2 IDs. Neither Pixgu nor any third parties can access, collect, or process the information exchanged between users. Even if someone collect the data, they will not be able to understand exchanged data without the encryption key stored in the user's browser.

- We limit required information. We only ask for what's necessary to create your account, maintain our services, and meet legal requirements.

- We don't sell your personal information. Our business is based on purchases and donates, not selling your data to third parties.

## The information we collect

We collect certain information when you use Pixgu. This includes information you provide to us, information we collect automatically, and information we receive from other sources.

- ##### Information you provide to us

  - **Account information:** When you create a Pixgu account, you only need to choose a username. If you're creating an account via an OAuth provider, depending on the provider, we may receive additional information such as your email address and/or phone number, but this is not required or stored for account creation on our website.

  [Other "Information you provide to us" subsections remain the same]

- ##### Information we collect automatically

  - **Access the services:** We collect some information to make accessing the service possible. This includes:

    - IP address
    - Operating system information
    - Browser information
    - Device settings, such as your language
    - Location data derived from IP address

  - **Information about your use of website:** For example, we collect log and event information related to how and when you use our services (such as the pages you visit).

  - **Other information that we collect automatically:** When you take certain actions on other sites, we may receive information about you. For example, when we advertise for Pixgu on third party platforms, if you click on the ad, we may receive information about which ad you saw and on which platform. Similarly, we may also receive certain information when you click on a referral link, such as which website you came from. We may receive information from cookies and similar technologies.

- ##### Information we receive from other sources

  We may receive information about you from other sources, including from other users and third parties, and combine that information with the other information we have about you. For example, if you interact with our social media account on another platform, we may receive certain information about you like your username on that platform.

## How we use your information

- **To provide you with the services:** We use your information to provide you with the Pixgu services. For example, We use your account information to connect you to game rooms. When you enable optional features (like connecting your Pixgu account to other platforms), we use information from those services to power the feature (like displaying what song you are listening to on another service).

- **To maintain a positive community:** We are committed to making Pixgu a fun, safe, and inclusive environment for all players. To achieve this, we use your information to monitor and take action against users and content that violate our service. Such as responding to player reports, detecting fraud and cheating attempts and proactively scanning user-generated content for inappropriate or harmful material

- **To contact you:** We use your information to contact you in connection with your account, such as contact you about important product or policy changes and to send you information about products you have purchased.

- **To provide technical support:** We use your information to respond to your issues and provide assistance. This helps us assist you with various aspects of Pixgu, such as: Addressing issues with balances, investigating bugs or glitches, clarifying rules or gameplay features and resolving account-related concerns

- **To report on our business performance:** We use your information to track the fundamental metrics of our business, to perform financial reporting, to respond to regulatory obligations, and to debug billing issues.

- **To protect our services:** We use your information to keep our services secure, to prevent misuse, and to enforce our service and other policies against users who violate them.

- **To protect someone’s vital interests:** We may collect or share personal data if we think someone’s life is in danger—for example, to help resolve an urgent medical situation.

## Data retention

Pixgu maintains a data retention policy whereby guest accounts and their associated information are automatically removed following 14 days of inactivity. Accounts authenticated through OAuth providers are permanently maintained and not subject to automatic deletion.

## Deleting account

You can delete your account via the Account Settings menu. Deleting your account permanently deletes identifying information and anonymizes other data as described in this privacy policy under data retention heading.

## Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website for the following purposes:

- **Essential Cookies:** These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off. They are used for user authentication and maintaining your session.

- **Analytics Cookies:** We use these cookies to collect information about how visitors use our website, helping us improve our services and user experience.
